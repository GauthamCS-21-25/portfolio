Video Calling Application

During the COVID-19 pandemic, many companies are finding a variety of ways to communicate with their customers. Organizations may use teledetailing/telemarketing through email, social media or other third-party functions for video conferencing. The main purpose of video conferencing is to enable face-to-face communication between two or more people in different locations. It is a popular alternative to phone conferencing for businesses and provides individual users with an inexpensive means of communication with distant friends and family.

I have created the application GCall which offers individual users to video call their near and dear ones and businesses officials to conduct meetings. I built this using Java in Android Studio. I have used Jitsi Meet API to enable some of the features and provide an end-to-end encryption security.
